Equipment Tracking System

Introduction

Welcome to the Equipment Tracking System, a project designed to keep track of all equipment at our institute. With this system, we can easily monitor the inventory of equipment, including computers, projectors, and other devices, ensuring that we know where everything is and that we have an up-to-date record of each item's status.
